The Timee Story

Time is the most precious thing we have. Yet we give it away so easily — to notifications, to noise, to the relentless rush of more.

At Timee, we believe in slowing down. In finding beauty in the natural world. In surrounding ourselves with objects that carry meaning.

Inspired by the bee — nature's most intentional creator — and the crystal — formed over millions of years deep within the earth — Timee brings you small reminders to breathe, to be present, to reconnect with what matters.
